my name is greg o’connell.
this is my personal website. i primarily use it to upload recent images that i captured, gifs i have made, projects i am working on, and occasionally upload celebrity crushes, i.e. hemingway & sagan.

i made this website to showcase the items and everyday events i find interesting. most of the images and .gif(s) i have taken and created. this site also acts and serves as my resumé. since late 2012 i have been working for social print studio. i joined as a photographer at first capturing images of taxidermy. then moved on to be their full time product photographer producing content for both our e-commerce site and social media. i then started the frame dept. essentially adding framed prints as a product to our website. i would be in a damp basement blocks away from Cal, in Berkeley printing and framing the few orders we would receive. Now over 10 thousand frames go out each year! Currently I have transferred to the creative wing or tentacle of SPS, we simply call the “agency.” we work alongside clients and produce on the spot printing of t-shirts, stickers and instagram photos.
