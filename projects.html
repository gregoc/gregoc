past projects


onhub makers
OnHub is a router from Google that provides fast wi-fi. artists, designers, and makers from around the world were invited to create their own OnHub shells. i produced the photography and stop motion of the shells.
role: producer / photographer



space selfie
i blended my intrigue of space, satellite-imagery, and drones with the cultural phenomenon of selfies into a 15sec video. naming it space selfie. it is a fun concept: from space to your face! currently it is a permanent installation at facebook hq in building 15. this was the original concept video. hi mom!



glaze
concept video i created to be used as a potential activation at an event. it is a fairly simple concept, uses minimal hardware, and the content {ink drops} can be interchangeable. i am working on adding ocean marine life footage to be overlaid. inspired by a dream.



adweek in nyc
i had the amazing opportunity to not only take and instantly share portraits of individuals who attended adweek but to also capture video portraits of them. our concept was to give people professional quality portraits to be used as their profile image. along with capturing their photo i would video record 10 sec of them that would be then be displayed on multiple screens.



photographer
i have been the in-house photographer, photo editor and art director at social print studio for the past 3 years. it can be challenging taking photographs of photographs ;)



frame dept.
at social print studio we wanted to offer a new product to our customers; frames! i spearheaded the projected creating the multiple layouts, finding the proper sized frames, printing and shipping logistics and eventually printing framing and shipping to customers from a basement in a house located in berkeley, ca. you can still order them here. the basement had mold so we moved the production to a warehouse in soma.
